Strawberry Horchata

Combines fragrant strawberries with the rich goodness of classic horchata.

Yields 10 to 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry long-grain white rice
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick, broken up
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugat
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, rinsed

Recipie

  1. Pour the rice, cinnamonstick, and water into the blender, and blend on high until the rice begins to break down, about 3 minutes. Cover and let the rice water sit at room temperature for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  2. Pour the rice water into a large pitcher, through a fine-mesh strainer. Discard the rice and cinnamon bits.
  3. Stir the milk, vanilla, cinnamon and sugar into the rice water
  4. Trim the tops of the strawberries and add them to the blender. Blend on high until completely smooth. Strain into the horchata through a fine-mesh strainer to remove the seeds.
  5. Stir the horchata until well combined, and give it a taste and adjust sugar or cinnamon as needed. Chill until ready to serve.
    6. Pour over ice and enjoy!
